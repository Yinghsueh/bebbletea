About Us

Established in 1998, our beverage shop specializes in crafting premium drinks with fresh tea leaves. Our signature milk tea blends the richness of tea with creamy milk, brewed to perfection.

Committed to freshness and quality, we source the finest ingredients for a consistently delightful experience. Join us for a journey of flavor and tradition.

Our Profiosion

Originating from Taiwan, pearl milk tea harmoniously blends the refreshing essence of tea with creamy milk, often complemented by chewy tapioca pearls or other toppings.

With a myriad of flavors and customizable options, this beloved beverage offers a unique and versatile taste experience, perfect for any season or preference.
